Sweet Roll Recipe

They're the most stolen items in Tamriel - apparently, even dragons can't resist the allure of a sweet roll. But good luck finding one that hasn't been swiped!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour (stolen, of course)
  • 1/4 cup of sugar (loot from the local pantry)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt (borrowed from a nearby alchemy shop)
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter (appropriated from a noble's kitchen)
  • 2 tablespoons of stolen vanilla extract
  • 1 large stolen egg
  • A pinch of cinnamon (swiped from a local market)
  • Sweet, sweet oblivion

Recipe instructions:

  1. Preheat your stolen oven to 350 degrees, and line your stolen baking sheet with parchment paper (or, you know, borrow it).0
  2. In a stolen bowl, combine the pilfered flour, sugar, and salt. Mix them as silently as a thief in the night.
  3. Cut the ill-gotten butter into small cubes and sneakily incorporate it into the flour mixture until it resembles breadcrumbs. Be as stealthy as a Khajiit on a moonlit night.
  4. Whisk the stolen vanilla extract into the egg, then stir this concoction into the flour mixture. Blend it all together until you've created the perfect alibi for your sweet rolls.
  5. Divide the dough into small, mischievous portions and roll them into balls, like a master thief rolling dice for the ultimate loot. Place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Sprinkle just a hint of the pinched cinnamon on top, for that extra aromatic distraction.
  7. Bake your sweet rolls in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until they're a lovely shade of golden brown. Keep a lookout for any nosy guards; you don't want them to catch you red-handed.
  8. Once they're done, let them cool, but be sure to indulge while they're still warm. Serve with a sly grin, and remember, if anyone asks, you didn't steal these delicious sweet rolls - they were a gift from a friendly Khajiit.
